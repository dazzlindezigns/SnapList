# SnapList — Demo Video Script
## 60–90 seconds · Phone screen record + voiceover

---

### SHOT 1 — Hook (0:00–0:08)
**What to record:** You at your craft table, products in front of you, looking at your phone like you're about to list something.

**You say (casual, direct):**
> "Okay so if you're a maker and you're still writing your Etsy listings by hand... liiisten. Let me show you something."

---

### SHOT 2 — The Problem (0:08–0:15)
**What to record:** Scroll through a long, blank listing form on Etsy or just stare at a blank notes app.

**You say:**
> "I used to spend 30–45 minutes per listing. Title, description, keywords, all of it. Every. Single. Time. That ends today."

---

### SHOT 3 — Open SnapList (0:15–0:20)
**What to record:** Open snaplist.danicreatesit.com on your phone. Show the landing page briefly.

**You say:**
> "This is SnapList. Watch how fast this goes."

---

### SHOT 4 — Upload the photo (0:20–0:30)
**What to record:** Tap "Try it free," upload a photo of one of your actual products from your camera roll.

**You say:**
> "I'm just grabbing a photo straight from my phone. No fancy setup, no photoshoot. Just... my product."

---

### SHOT 5 — Hit generate (0:30–0:35)
**What to record:** Select your platform (Etsy), tap the Generate button. Show the loading spinner briefly.

**You say:**
> "I hit generate. That's literally it."

---

### SHOT 6 — The listing appears (0:35–0:50)
**What to record:** Scroll through the generated title, description, and keywords slowly so viewers can read them.

**You say:**
> "In about 30 seconds — a full SEO-optimized title, three paragraphs of description, thirteen keywords, category, price suggestion. Ready to copy and paste. Done."

---

### SHOT 7 — The mockups (0:50–1:05)
**What to record:** Scroll down to the AI mockups. Show all 10 slowly. Let them sink in.

**You say:**
> "And THEN — ten professional product mockups. Different scenes, different vibes. All from that one photo I took at my kitchen table."

---

### SHOT 8 — The close (1:05–1:20)
**What to record:** Face cam. You holding your product. Smile.

**You say:**
> "This is SnapList. It's $19. One time. No subscription, no monthly fees, yours forever. If you sell handmade products online, this is for you. Link in bio."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Tone:** Real, warm, a little excited — not salesy. You're a maker showing other makers something that actually works.

**Music:** Lo-fi or upbeat background track in CapCut. Keep it low so voice is clear.

**Captions:** Turn on auto-captions in CapCut. They convert people scrolling with sound off.

**Text overlays to add in CapCut:**
- Shot 4: *"Just a phone photo"*
- Shot 6: *"30 seconds"* + *"SEO-optimized"*
- Shot 7: *"10 AI mockups"* + *"From ONE photo"*
- Shot 8: *"$19 one-time"* + *"snaplist.danicreatesit.com"*

**Best platforms to post:**
- TikTok (post first)
- Instagram Reels (repost)
- Facebook — your crafter group specifically
- Pinterest video pin (crafters are heavy Pinterest users)
